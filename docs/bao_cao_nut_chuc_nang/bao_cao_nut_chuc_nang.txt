BAO CAO NUT VA CHUC NANG NUT - DU AN LOTTO
Ngay lap: 2026-07-09

Pham vi ra soat:
- frontend/vietlott-web.html: nut tinh tren giao dien.
- frontend/vietlott-web-core.js: bind su kien click/change va dieu huong UI.
- frontend/vietlott-web-data.js: luong du doan, live data, prize panel, data table.
- frontend/vietlott-web-stats.js: thong ke, thong ke V2, dashboard, phan tich.

Ghi chu:
- "Handler" la ham/chuc nang JS chinh duoc goi khi bam nut.
- Cac nut sinh dong bang JS duoc ghi theo selector data-*.
- Cac nut placeholder/chua gan click rieng duoc danh dau ro.

16. Tong ket nhanh cac nut can luu y
============================================================

Nut co chuc nang day du:
- Dang nhap/dang ky/admin recovery.
- Menu home/deposit/data/wheel.
- Nap tien PayPal.
- Vong quay va doi luot quay.
- Live update, live history, data table.
- Du doan thuong/Vip, lich su du doan.
- Thong ke gan day, Thong Ke V2, dashboard, phan tich.
- Quan ly tai khoan admin.

Nut hien dang la placeholder hoac chi hien trang thai:
- Side menu: Thong tin tai khoan, Nhat ki hoat dong, Lich su hoan tien, Cap bac Tai khoan, Chuong trinh Affiliates.
- lottoDashboardStatusBtn: chi la nut/indicator trang thai, khong co click handler rieng.

Ket luan:
- Giao dien co he thong nut lon va phan lon da duoc gan handler ro rang.
- Cac nut quan trong deu map ve ham JS/API tuong ung.
- Neu can phat trien tiep, nen uu tien hoan thien cac side-link placeholder hoac doi chung thanh item disabled de nguoi dung khong bam nham.
